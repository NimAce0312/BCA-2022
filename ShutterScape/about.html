GET TO KNOW ME

Hi!

My name is Bikash Subedi and I am a Nepali male photographer. I specialize in landscape, nature and portrait photography, and my work has been featured in various exhibitions and galleries.

I have over a decade of experience in the field and I have developed a keen eye for detail and composition. I am able to bring a fresh perspective to my photographs.
I have traveled extensively across Nepal to capture the beauty and diversity of the country and my work showcases the rich culture and natural landscapes of Nepal.
I am also a mentor to aspiring photographers and conduct workshops and photography tours to share my knowledge and experience.
